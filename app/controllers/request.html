What kind of work do you want done? *select all that apply*
Tuning

A tuning is the most basic service technicians perform on a piano. Usually this service can include minor repairs and adjustments to the piano that improve it's playability/stability.

Pitch Raise

If a piano has not been serviced for 2+ years it will most likely need a pitch raise before it can be properly tuned.

Repairs

Potential repairs that a piano may need vary greatly. You may have "sticking keys", "keys that make a weird sound", or a pedal that doesn't work they way it should. Indicate in the comment field below any specific concerns.

Internal Cleaning

Remove years of dust, spider webs, and foreign objects in a way that is gentle and safe for the piano.

Regulation

An extensive overhaul of your piano's action that completely evens out each key's weight, feel, depression depth, volume, and performance.

Purchase Consult

You wouldn't buy a car without having it checked by a mechanic you trust. Do the same with a piano. Piano technicians can help you know what to look for, what to avoid, and advise you on fair prices for your area.

Estimate

Anticipating a large repair that you need to know how to budget for? Estimates can help you know what to expect.

Appraisal

Find out your piano's current value and have it documented.
